Drainage Awareness Program

Parañaque City

Working Together for a Flood-Free Community

About the Issue

Parañaque City experiences flooding especially during heavy rains. Many drainage systems become clogged due to waste and lack of maintenance. This causes inconvenience, property damage, and health risks to residents.

Causes

  • Improper garbage disposal in streets and canals
  • Plastic waste blocking drainage systems
  • Insufficient drainage maintenance
  • Rapid urbanization and construction

Effects

  • Flooding in low-lying barangays
  • Spread of water-borne diseases
  • Traffic congestion during rainfall
  • Damage to homes and businesses

Implementations & Solutions

  • Regular community clean-up drives
  • Strict enforcement of waste segregation
  • Improvement of drainage infrastructure
  • Public awareness campaigns in barangays
  • Encouraging residents to report clogged canals
